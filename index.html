About Me


I started my editing career Junior year of high school. I took a Film & Broadcasting class because I needed an art credit to graduate.

Most of my basis for editing came from that class. I learned how to work with Adobe Photoshop and Premiere. Our first assignment my professor said I went over the top and made it really cool. That's when I knew this is something I really liked doing, and could potentially see myself doing in the future.

After a a year of the class I knew I was more interested in the special effects side of filmmaking, and I spent the next couple years teaching myself through Youtube videos and tutorials.

I'm now majoring in Media Aesthetics at NKU in hopes to advance my skills in this field. I'm going to minor in business so that I can have the knowledge to start my own company if I need to.

My dream is to take the skills I have to Universal Studios and work there, but realistically I hope to maybe make advertisements or videos for companies.
